    Introduction to MATLAB

    TBA • DCL L440

    This workshop targets students with little programming experience (in general) or with no prior exposure to MATLAB. We will conduct a hands-on walkthrough of what MATLAB has to offer as a foundation for later tutorials throughout the semester.

    We will cover the following topics:

    • Variables, functions, sequences, dictionaries
    • Libraries and modules
    • Loops, logic, and conditionals
    • Input/output, files

    (If you don't know what any or all of these are, don't worry! We will explain them.)

    There is no sign-up for this series—walk-ins are welcome and encouraged! However, if you are not an engineering student, you will need an EWS login to use the computer lab, so please email Neal Davis (contact information at bottom) at least 24 hours in advance.
